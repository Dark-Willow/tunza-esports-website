About Us

Shaping the future of Kenya’s esports through community, competition, and opportunity.

We Unite To Make Esports Great In Kenya

Tunza Esports is committed to growing esports across institutions by educating, organizing events, and building competitive communities. We are actively involved in developing the esports ecosystem and its surroundings.

  • Organizing high-quality esports tournaments across institutions.
  • Building and connecting gaming communities in Kenya.
  • Empowering students through mentorship and career pathways in esports.
  • Promoting inclusivity and accessibility in competitive gaming.
  • Expanding beyond universities to high schools and colleges.
  • Creating solutions for smooth tournament management.
  • Developing esports infrastructure and local opportunities.
  • Driving awareness of esports as a viable industry in Africa.
