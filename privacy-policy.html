Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10/08/2025

Tunza Esports LTD (“Tunza Esports,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you contact us or make purchases from our shop.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

This information is collected only when you:

  • Submit an enquiry through our contact form.
  • Make a purchase through the Tunza Esports Shop.

2. Payments

Payments are processed securely through Flutterwave.

  • We do not store or have access to your card or bank account details.
  • Flutterwave may collect additional payment information to process your transaction in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to your enquiries.
  • Process and deliver your orders.
  • Communicate important updates related to your purchases or queries.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We store your personal information securely and take reasonable measures to protect it from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share it if:

  • Required by law.
  • Necessary to complete your order (e.g., sharing delivery details with courier services).

6. Your Rights

You may request that we update or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us.
